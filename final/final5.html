Roseville California has a Topgolf and is very close to Sacramento, which is the home of the Sacramento Kings. You could golf at Topgolf and watch the Sacramento Kings game on the televisions at the bar. You could also take a short drive over to the arena to watch the game in person. The Roseville area is full of Kings fans and golf fans. We have a bunch of different golf courses and the Sacramento Kings are very loyal.
